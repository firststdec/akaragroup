Ingredients for 10 servings
All-purpose flour 3 cups | 390g
Baking powder 1 tbsp | 12g
Baking soda 1 tsp | 7g
Salt, kosher 2 tsp | 8g
Butter 1 cup | 8oz
Whole Milk Plain Greek Yogurt 1 cup | 8oz
Milk, whole 1 cup | 250g
Cheddar, sharp, grated 1 cup | 250g
Chives, sliced ½ cup | 28g
Akara Jumbo Eggs 3 Eggs | 100g
Step1
Preheat oven to 400ºF.
Step2
Preheat Combine dry ingredients and pulse in food processor. Add in butter cubes and pulse until coarse meal forms.
Step3
Add to wet ingredients and fold, being careful not to overwork dough.
Roll out dough to 1” thickness and cut dough in half. Take one half of dough and stack it on top of the other half for tall flaky layers.
Step4
Roll dough out again to 1” and cut out 10 biscuits with 3” circular dough cutter. Alternatively, cut squares for additional yield.
Step5
Place biscuits on half sheet tray lined with parchment paper.
Step6
Chill in freezer for 10 minutes, remove, and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until done.
